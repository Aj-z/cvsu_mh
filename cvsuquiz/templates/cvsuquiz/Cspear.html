College of Sports, Physical Education and Recreation

Bachelor of Physical Education

The Bachelor of Physical Education program delves into the principles of human movement, physical activity, and the role of sports in health and education. Students study anatomy, physiology, kinesiology, and teaching methodologies while exploring the cultural and social dimensions of physical activity, gaining a comprehensive understanding of physical education’s impact on individuals and communities.

This program fosters advanced skills in teaching, coaching, and fitness training, along with expertise in program design and implementation for various populations. Students develop critical thinking and leadership abilities, equipping them to promote health and wellness effectively while addressing societal needs through physical education and sports.

Graduates can pursue careers as educators, coaches, fitness trainers, or recreational program coordinators. Opportunities also exist in corporate wellness, community health promotion, and specialized fields like adaptive physical education. The program’s emphasis on versatility and innovation prepares graduates for diverse and dynamic roles in the fitness and education sectors.

Bachelor of Science in Exercise And Sport Science

focuses on the science of human performance, exercise, and sports management. Students explore biomechanics, sports psychology, exercise physiology, and nutrition, gaining a scientific perspective on optimizing athletic performance and promoting lifelong physical activity.

This program develops advanced knowledge in sports science, training design, and injury prevention while fostering research and analytical skills. Students gain practical experience through internships and hands-on learning, preparing them to apply evidence-based practices in enhancing physical performance and well-being.

Graduates are equipped for careers in sports science, athletic training, strength and conditioning, or wellness coaching. They can work in fitness centers, rehabilitation clinics, sports organizations, or research institutions. With the flexibility to pursue remote roles like online coaching or consulting, the program offers adaptability and relevance in the growing fields of health and sports science.
