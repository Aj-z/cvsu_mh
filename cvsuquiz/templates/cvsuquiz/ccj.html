Bachelor of Science In Criminology

The Bachelor of Science in Criminology program examines the causes, prevention, and control of crime through a comprehensive study of criminal behavior, law enforcement, and the justice system.

Students gain in-depth knowledge of criminological theories, forensic science, criminal law, and investigative techniques while exploring ethical and societal implications. This program hones critical thinking, analytical, and leadership skills, preparing students to address crime-related challenges effectively.

Graduates are equipped for careers in law enforcement, forensic analysis, corrections, security management, and criminological research, with opportunities to contribute to public safety and justice.

Bachelor of Science in Industrial Security Management

The Bachelor of Science in Industrial Security Management program focuses on safeguarding people, assets, and information through modern security practices and risk management strategies.Students study principles of physical security, cybersecurity, crisis management, and threat assessment, alongside ethical and legal considerations in industrial security.

The program emphasizes leadership, strategic planning, and technological proficiency, equipping graduates to address security challenges in dynamic environments.

Graduates can pursue roles as security consultants, risk analysts, corporate security managers, or specialists in cybersecurity and emergency preparedness, contributing to organizational resilience and safety.
